7 Study-Friendly Coffee Shops in Columbia

Are you a student looking for a coffee shop to study at, but you’re not sure where to begin? Look no further! This page provides a list of study-friendly coffee shops for students in Columbia. Whether you’re searching for a spot that offers food, or you’re sick of being indoors and want to study somewhere with outdoor seating, there’s a java shop catered to your needs.

Acola Coffee Company

300 N 10th St Suite 100
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 447-8050
Visit Acola's website

Acola Coffee Company offers three stories of study spots, outdoor seating, and a crepery for students looking to snack while studying.

The Grind Coffee House - South

4603 John Garry Dr #1
Columbia, MO
65203
(573) 447-3333
Visit The Grind's website

The Grind Coffee House has three locations in Columbia for students looking for a coffee shop near them. Their south location is the largest of the shops after their recent expansion of indoor study spots.

Fretboard Coffee

1016 Artist Aly
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 227-2233
Visit Fretboard Coffee's website

Fretboard Coffee provides a cozy atmosphere for students looking to study under vintage lights and comfy couches.

La Calle 8 Café

214 S 8th St
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 825-8960
Visit La Calle 8 Café's website

La Calle 8 Café is a Latin-owned and run coffee shop located on 8th street, readily available for Mizzou students looking to fit in a quick study session between classes without having to leave campus.

Lakota Coffee Company - South

3111 Green Meadows Way
Columbia, MO
65203
(573) 499-9901
Visit Lakota Coffee Company's website

Lakota Coffee Company has three locations in Columbia. For those looking to study outdoors, their south location offers an enclosed back patio with a fountain, providing a calm atmosphere.

Shortwave Coffee

29 S 9th St.
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 214-0880
Visit Shortwave Coffee's website

Shortwave Coffee has two downtown locations in Columbia. Their largest location on 9th street offers a variety of seating options, from tables, booths, and stools facing windows with a view of downtown Columbia.

Toasty Goat Coffee Co.

515 S Scott Blvd Ste 101
Columbia, MO
65203
(573) 607-9015
Visit Toasty Goat Coffee's website

Toasty Goat Coffee Co. is filled with a plethora of hanging plants for those looking to study in nature without facing the heat or cold of the outdoors.
